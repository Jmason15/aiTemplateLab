🧰 Build your own template library

The Template Lab group comes pre-loaded with three tools for building your own AI prompt library — just run them in your AI tool and paste the result back.

  • Template Builder — Describe what you want a prompt to do and the AI designs a complete, ready-to-use template.
  • Template Group Generator — Tell the AI your job title and it generates a full set of templates for every task you'd regularly need help with.
  • Workflow Generator — Describe a multi-step goal and the AI breaks it into a sequence of templates, one per stage.

Click the green Build a New Template button in the sidebar to get started.

💾 How your work is saved

Your templates save automatically to your browser as you work — no account or internet needed. They persist across page refreshes and restarts on the same device and browser.

To back up or share your templates, use Export / Share in the sidebar. This lets you download or share individual templates, a whole group, or your entire workspace as a JSON file.

To restore on another device, click Import and drop the file in.

⚠️ Don't lose your templates

Browser storage can be wiped when you clear browser data, switch browsers, or use a different computer. Export your workspace regularly so you always have a backup.

Use Export / Share → Everything in the sidebar, or File → Save Workspace in the menu bar. It takes seconds and protects everything you have built.
